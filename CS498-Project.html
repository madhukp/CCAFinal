Introduction


Objective:

North American emergency communication center consists of a team of individuals who are tasked with responding to emergency calls to the number 9-1-1. Initial call-takers and communications supervisor are main roles within the center. The call-takers are responsible for calming the callers, in processing information on the nature of emergency and in dispatching appropriate first-responders like Paramedics or Firefighters. One of the main task of communications supervisor is to regularly track the volume and statistics on the calls and use it to better plan and resource the dispatchers and first-responders so as to effectively deploy assistance and service emergency calls. The volume of calls received within a single city or state is so huge and grows so rapidly that it has been near impossible to draw insights with traditional technologies. As part of this project, we intend to solve this problem by offering a Big Data Analytics solution coupled with machine learning and visualization utilities to draw valuable insights from the calls dataset and help better equip the emergency response teams.
